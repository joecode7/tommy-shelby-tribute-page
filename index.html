Thomas "Tommy" Shelby


Notable Events:

  • 1919
    • - Inadvertantly comes into possesion of a large shipment of miltary guns and ammunition from the BSA (Birmingham Small Arms Co. ). Shelby is investigated by an Inspector Campbell from Ireland. Aiding Campbell in his investigation is Grace; posing as a barmaid with the voice of an angel. Shelby is immeadiately smitten with Grace. Eventually, Shelby finds out Grace is a spy and they go their seperate ways. In the meanwhile, Shelby gains control of the horsetrack, with the hopes of someday legitimizing his family's empire.
  • 1922
    • - Shelby expands the family empire to London; with the help of local "bread maker", Alfie Solomons. Shelby also finds his long-lost cousin, Michael Gray, and appoints him the position of accountant for the family. Inspector Campbell is murdered by the family matriarch, Polly. Grace returns and tell Shelby that she is in love with him
  • 1924
    • - Grace and Tommy are now married with one son, Charles. Grace is later shot and killed from a member of the Italian Mafia after being provoked by Tommy's brother, John. Shelby throws himself into buisness to help forget his grief. He begins contact with Russians. Multiple members of the family are arrested; although they are reassured by Shelby that he has made a deal with very powerful people to release them.
  • 1926
    • - All members of the family are released from prison, however they alienate themselves from each other for roughly 1 year. The feud between the Shelby family and the Italian Mafia continues, which forces the family back together. The Shelby family obviously win this war.
  • 1929
    • - Shelby is still struggling with the death of his wife Grace. He has however remarried. He marries his long-time friend, confidant, and go-to prostitute, Lizzie. Shelby works his way into the upper-echelon of politics and is a respected speaker in the House of Commons. Shelby forms an alliance with rising Nazi superstar, Oswald Mosley. Shelby plans to undermine Mosley and his beliefs. On the night Shelby plans to assainate Mosley; the plan is thwarted by a stupid mistake from youngest brother Finn. That is all we know.... for now.
